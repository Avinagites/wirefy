What is it?

Wirefy is a browser based responsive wireframe tool, which allows you to create functional wireframes using standard elements. Think of it as the foundation for your content to be built on. This allows you to focus on the content rather than the design to get faster sign off. Wirefy is not a one-solution answer.

Who is for?

For anyone who is concerned with designing with content first as the base for their responsive design. If you are new to responsive design, this tool will help you get up and running faster. Start building flexible wireframes without the headache of working through countless calculations. Instead focus on the content and flow.

Why use it?

Creating static wireframes can be great but sometimes clients just don't understand how they function or change based on various devices. Building functional wireframes will help you communicate with clients better and work as a foundation for your fluid designs without losing sight of the importance of the content.

How does it work?

Here is a brief look at the process to create your wireframes.

  • Plan

    Architect structure through sketches & organize your content quickly. This is a great way to quickly test out ideas and build a strategy that will focus your wireframes.

  • Structure

    Build specific layout designs & guidelines for your content. Functionality can sometimes break the flow of a site. Some ideas are better in static form but don't work in the real world. Get through these iterations faster.

  • Communicate

    Discuss foundation and functionality of wireframes with clients by showing them working examples of how the site will work. Drive conversations towards more productive collaboration with clients.

  • Deliver

    Get approval on your wireframes faster. Once these have been approved you can use your wireframe builds to design on top of. Cut down your timelines and be happy that your clients will have a better understanding about the final product.
